Everything is taken care of -- even the kids -- so you can enjoy your dream vacation: windsurfing in the morning, tennis in the afternoon, poolside cocktails, romantic walks by the sea.The Olympia Aqua Park is a brand-new exclusive water park in Greece of 20.000m2 in the Activity Park of the Grecotel Olympia Riviera Resort. It is the largest water park in a resort in Greece. There are heart-stopping waterslides and rides as well as water park play opportunities for the little ones.


Their rides include: Looping rocket - A high adrenaline ride, Space hole - One of the largest rides for speed riding, Rafting slide - unforgettable ride from the top of the slide tower, Flying boats - high speed, special effects fuelled ride, Multisurf - Test your nerves at this exhilarating mega-slide. Also offered are relaxing seating areas and bar in the sun speckled patios embraced by a natural pine woods.
